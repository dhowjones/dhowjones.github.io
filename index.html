Hello world!

I'm Roshni, a historical researcher, writer, digital humanist, programmer, and visual artist currently obtaining my MSc in Global Digital Humanities, generously supported by the University of St Andrews' St Leonards Digital Education Scholarship.

Whether writing articles on aviation history in the Persian Gulf for afikra, developing games to promote engagement with the Indian Ocean's historical maritime trade routes, or live-coding visuals at Doha's grassroots events, my work explores themes of memory, identity, movement, migration, and cultural exchange, usually through technological mediums.

I'm always open to new collaborations—feel free to reach out via email or find me on LinkedIn.
